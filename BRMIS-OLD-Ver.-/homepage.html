About Us

BRMIS is designed to streamline the reservation, inventory, and management processes within a blood bank. We focus on improving efficiency, minimizing wastage of blood units, and providing real-time updates for technicians, admins, and heads.

Our mission is to ensure safe, quick, and organized blood transactions to save more lives and optimize blood bank operations.

Our Services

Inventory Management

Track available blood units with real-time updates to avoid shortages and manage stock effectively.

Reservation System

Reserve specific blood types for scheduled operations and emergencies, minimizing delays and shortages.

Barcode Tracking

Manage blood bags through barcodes for faster, error-free identification and better inventory control.
